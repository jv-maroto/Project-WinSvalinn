## Empresarial edition (separate product)

The **Empresarial edition** is a proprietary product, **not** included in this repository. It
adds, on top of the Free edition:

- Full CIS security audit (L1/L2) with one-click reversible remediation/hardening.
- Identity & system auditing: credentials, local users/groups, certificates, Wi-Fi, shares,
  scheduled tasks, system integrity, outbound firewall.
- Threat intelligence (VirusTotal), exportable reports and scheduled scans.

Activation uses an offline, Ed25519-signed license key (no server, no account). The license
**verifier** (public key) ships in the Free edition so the app can recognize a valid key; the
key **issuer** (private key) is not distributed.
